Grievance Workflow
1
Employee Scans QR
Opens the Grievance Resolution Portal on phone. Selects issue category in preferred language.
2
Concern Submitted
Employee fills in the grievance details and submits. No identity required — fully anonymous.
3
Dashboard Notified
Grievance Register updates in real-time. HRBP auto-assigned. Priority auto-set.
4
HRBP Takes Action
HRBP reviews, updates status (Open → WIP → Closed). Employee grievance tracked end-to-end.
5
Resolution Tracked
TAT monitored. Satisfaction score collected post-resolution. Full closure loop.
